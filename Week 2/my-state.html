My State

North Carolina

The beautiful state of North Carolina is located in the southeast region of the country bordered by Virginia, Georgia, South Carolina, and Tennessee.

It was founded as a state on November 21st 1789 as the 12th state.

North Carolina is defined by a wide range of elevations and landscapes. From west to east, North Carolina's elevation descends from the Appalachian Mountains to the Piedmont and Atlantic coastal plain

The capital is located in Raleigh, which is the states second most populous city. The most populous city is Charlotte, which has an estimated population of 2,569,213 people.

Speaking of populous cities. Located in North Carolina is the second largest military base in the world. Fort Bragg, home of the historica airborne unit "The 82nd Airborne" and also home of the U.S. Army Special Operations.
